COLOR FORMAT
------------
Use standard hex colors: #RRGGBB
Examples:
  #FFFFFF = White
  #000000 = Black
  #FF0000 = Red
  #00FF00 = Green
  #0000FF = Blue
  #FFD700 = Gold
  #515151 = Dark Gray (default shadow)

Note: GBA uses 15-bit color (5 bits per channel).
Colors will be automatically converted, but subtle
gradients may be lost. Stick to distinct colors.
